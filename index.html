<Hello World/>

My name is Adam Boissonneault I am an aspiring software developer. I am currently attending the Manitoba Institute of Trades and Technology taking their full stack devloper course. I have been learning about web developement for the last few years and have created many websites and web apps, including Hop Master 3D, Innermaps, Atlas Checklists, and Ninjas Unblocked Games (see below)

Hop Master 3D was a parkour simulator I made in grade 9, using Unity Engine. The objective of the game was to jump from building to building while shooting the enemies. Once you've shot all the enemies a portal would open where you could jump into to enter into the next level. I made this game with the help of Sehaj Brar.

Innermaps was an indoor mapping software, I built last year. It was built to help university students find their was around campuses. The software would map large indoor spaces like campuses, hospitals, and office buildings and would direct you from room to room. We thought that there were many mapping software for roads but none for large areas that are can be easily confused.

Atlas Checklists was a personal project a created to help remember the diffent checklists you would need to fly an airplane. For example when you would click on the checklists for the Cessna 152, all the checklists for that plane would be there including start-up, run-up, descent, landing, and all emergency checklists.

Ninjas unblocked games was a website that I helped build, I was a website to allow students such as ourselves to play different video games in class. This project was released 2 years ago and since then we have managed to have 10,655 visited to our website from students in the UK, USA, Australia, and Germany.
